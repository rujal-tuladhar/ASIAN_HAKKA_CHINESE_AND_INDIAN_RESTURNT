Welcome To Asian Hakka Chinese & Indian Restaurant

Asian Hakka Chinese & Indian Restaurant takes pride in providing exceptional customer service, as well as high-quality and consistent food and beverages. We provide traditional Nepalese, Hakka Chinese, and Indian dishes. Our chefs prepare original dishes with reverence and respect for the cuisine. We invite you to join along and take part in the adventure.
